About Me

I am a Data Analyst with over 9+ years of experience delivering analytics solutions across healthcare and logistics environments. My work focuses on transforming operational data into structured, decision-ready insights that improve performance and visibility.

That curiosity evolved into a career centered on building clarity from complexity — bridging operational reality with strategic insight through structured analysis and executive-ready reporting.
